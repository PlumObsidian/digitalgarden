# [BBC Radio 6 Music - Dream Time, A trip down memory lane… with Zakia](https://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/m002n8hk)


## A trip down memory lane… with Zakia

Zakia Sewell waves farewell to 2025 with a trip down memory lane, including some favourite releases from 2025 and a chance to relive some of our past guests’ dreams.

Zakia Sewell waves farewell to 2025 with a trip down memory lane, indulging in a bit of nostalgia for her final show of the year. She shares a selection of her favourite releases from the last 12 months, from the likes of Kokoroko, Sessa, keiyaA, Ancient Infinity Orchestra and more. We also get a chance to relive some of the magical dreams and dreambiences of special guests from 2025, including Beverly Glenn-Copeland, Shirley Collins and Suzanne Ciani.
