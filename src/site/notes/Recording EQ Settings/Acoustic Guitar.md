# Acoustic guitar

80-100Hz: Bottom / muddiness
100-240Hz: Body
100-400Hz: Boominess
800-2000Hz: Boxiness
2-5kHz: Presence
3-7kHz: Attack
5kHz and up: Brightness
8kHz and up: Brittleness
10kHz and up: Pick noise
