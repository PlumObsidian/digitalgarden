# Distorted Electric Guitar

50-60Hz: Mains hum
100Hz: Muddiness
150-250Hz: Warmth
240-500Hz: Fullness
400-1000Hz: Crunch
2-5kHz: Bite
6kHz and up: Sizzle
8kHz and up: Noise
